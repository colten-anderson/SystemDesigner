# Business Context

## Summary
Capture why the system exists and what business outcomes depend on it.

## Interview Prompts
- What core business purpose does this system serve?
- Who are the user groups and dependent teams?
- What value or risk does it represent?
- What SLAs/SLOs or contractual obligations apply?
- Who is the business owner and escalation chain?
- What regulatory, legal, or contractual drivers matter?
