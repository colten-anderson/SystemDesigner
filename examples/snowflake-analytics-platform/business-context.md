# Business Context — Snowflake Analytics Platform

## Purpose
Centralizes trusted analytical data to support finance close, product decisions, and operational reporting.

## Core outcomes
- Provides a governed semantic layer for key company KPIs.
- Delivers near-real-time event analytics with <30 minute freshness for priority domains.
- Reduces ad-hoc spreadsheet reconciliation for monthly close.

## Success metrics
- Freshness SLA met for critical marts >= 98% of runs.
- Data quality test pass rate >= 99.5%.
- Executive KPI dashboard availability >= 99.9%.
